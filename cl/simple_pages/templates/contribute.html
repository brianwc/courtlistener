Contribute

Overview

This is an open source and open data project. Its success hinges on the support of its users. You can help to make it better.

There are many ways to help. The easiest way is to give us your feedback via the contact form. If you notice something wrong or something great, let us know.

Programmers, we welcome your contributions to our code. We've also created a virtual machine, the Free Law Machine, that includes everything you need to get started working on our code.

Attorneys, if you are using this site and discover that something is worded wrong, looks wrong, or is just plain wrong, please let us know!

If you do marketing, we need your help too.

We're also looking for people to help with minor data cleanup and quality issues. If you're interested, drop us a line on the contact page.

Feature Planning

We do all of our planning in the open. If you want to see our priorities, simply look at our planning board.

Bugs

If you find any problems with CourtListener at all, please let us know! To file a bug, please go to our bug tracker, and fill out this form with as much detail about what you were doing and what happened when the system broke. This gives us invaluable feedback to keep things running properly.

Discuss

If you're interested in talking about the site, the project, or anything else, please visit our forum, and start a discussion. We're happy to hear from you.

Quick actions

  • See and monitor our planning board
  • File a bug in our bug tracker
  • Browse and contribute to the code
  • Contact us with your ideas
